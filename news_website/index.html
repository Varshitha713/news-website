BTS’ J-Hope begins 30-day
countdown to military discharge

Seoul: J-Hope is set to become the next BTS member to be discharged from the South Korean military, and his fans, aka the BTS ARMY, cannot wait. J-Hope recently took to Weverse to mark the 30-day countdown to his military discharge, which is scheduled for October 17, 2024. Shortly after his post, excited fans said they were glad he will soon complete his mandatory service and return to civilian life.



BTS' Jin reveals he has 'never been asked out', shocks fans: 'I don't know anyone'

TS’ Jin recently surprised his fans with a candid confession in a new episode of Run Jin, where he revealed that, despite being a member of the world’s one of the biggest boy bands, he has never been asked out on a date. The episode featured YouTuber Kwaktube, MMA fighter Kim Dong Hyun, and TXT member Yeonjun. While participating in one of the games, Jin shared this unexpected detail about his personal life.
